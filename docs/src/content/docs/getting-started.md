## Download

Talkdraft is available on the App Store for iPhone (iOS 26+).

## Sign In

Talkdraft supports three entry methods:

- **Apple** — fastest option, uses your Apple ID
- **Email Magic Link** — enter your email and continue from the sign-in link
- **Guest** — try the app without creating a full account first

Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth.

## Onboarding

After sign-in, new users go through a short onboarding flow:

1. Choose a **recording language**
2. Pick a few **starter categories**
3. Review the **trial/paywall** screen
4. Optionally enable **trial reminder notifications**

## First Steps

1. **Create your first note** — tap the record button in the toolbar to capture a voice memo, or the compose button to type a text note
2. **Let AI work** — after recording, your voice is transcribed automatically and a title is generated in the background
3. **Organize** — create categories (e.g., Ideas, Action Items, Journal) and assign notes to them
4. **Refine** — use AI Rewrite to clean up, formalize, or restyle any note

## Account Management

- **Delete account** — available in Settings. Schedules a 30-day grace period before permanent deletion. Can be canceled during the grace period.
- **Subscription** — start the introductory trial when eligible, then continue with Pro for full access.
