Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Talkdraft → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

How do I delete my account?

In Talkdraft, go to Settings → Delete Account. Your account will be scheduled for deletion with a 30-day grace period. After 30 days, all data is permanently removed.

Can I restore my subscription on a new device?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID and tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen.

What languages are supported for transcription?

Talkdraft supports 49 languages for transcription. You can set your preferred language in Settings or use Auto-detect.
