Need help?

If you run into a problem with Talkdraft, email support@talkdraft.app. Please include the device you’re using, your iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Account & billing

  • Manage your subscription from Settings > Manage Subscription in the app.
  • Delete your account from Settings > Delete Account. Account deletion includes a 30-day grace period.
  • Restore purchases from the paywall if your subscription is not showing correctly.
